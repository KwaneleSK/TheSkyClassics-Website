Kwazi Buthelezi

Biography

Kwazi Buthelezi, the visionary founder of The Sky Classics, hails from the rich cultural heartland of KwaZulu Natal, specifically Ulundi. Growing up in the vibrant community of Ngwelezane Township in Empangeni, Kwazi was immersed in the sounds and rhythms of his heritage, which profoundly influenced his musical journey.

His passion for music blossomed during his time at the University of Zululand, where he not only pursued his studies but also became an integral part of the University Choir. It was here, amidst the harmonies and camaraderie of fellow musicians, that Kwazi's dream of forming a band began to take shape. Drawing inspiration from the diverse musical talents of his peers, he founded The Sky Classics, a group dedicated to celebrating and innovating the sounds of their roots.

Kwazi's leadership and artistic vision have been pivotal in shaping The Sky Classics into a beacon of musical excellence. His dedication to his craft and unwavering commitment to his community have not only elevated the group but have also fostered a deep connection with audiences, resonating with the stories and experiences that reflect their shared heritage.

Through The Sky Classics, Kwazi Buthelezi continues to inspire others, proving that music is not just a form of entertainment but a powerful means of cultural expression and unity.
